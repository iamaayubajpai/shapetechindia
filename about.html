ABOUT US

Engineering trust into every structure we build.

COMPANY INTRODUCTION

Engineering trust into every structure we build.

ShapeTech India Contractors is a full-service engineering, construction and solar EPC company headquartered in Uttar Pradesh, executing projects across residential, commercial, industrial and government sectors.

What began as a regional civil contracting outfit has grown into a pan-India execution partner — without losing the site-level discipline that earned our first government tender.

MISSION

Deliver infrastructure that performs for decades, not just at handover.

We exist to translate approved drawings into structures that are safe, on-budget and built to outlast their warranty period.

VISION

To be Uttar Pradesh's most trusted mid-size EPC contractor, recognised nationally by 2030.

We aim to be the contractor government bodies and private developers call first when the timeline is tight and the tolerance for error is zero.

CORE VALUES
